AI Explanation

Photosynthesis is the process by which green plants, algae, and some bacteria make their own food using sunlight. They absorb light through a pigment called chlorophyll, which is found in their leaves. During photosynthesis, plants take in carbon dioxide (CO₂) from the air and water (H₂O) from the soil.

With the help of sunlight, they convert these into glucose, which is a type of sugar the plant uses for energy and growth.

A by-product of this process is oxygen (O₂), which is released into the atmosphere and is essential for humans and animals to breathe. In simple terms: Sunlight + Water + Carbon dioxide → Glucose + Oxygen
